SkyHub Crew — Static Design Mock

Static HTML rendering of the Expo/RN crew app. Pixel-accurate token mirror. Hand off to Stitch / Claude design / Figma to iterate.

Two columns per screen. A — Current (solid cards) what's on device today. B — VisionOS glass proposed: heavier wallpaper scrim, BlurView cards, hairline edge.
Phone frames are 390 × 844 (iPhone 14 Pro). Screens are scrollable inside the frame.
